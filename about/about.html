WELCOME! I'AM GLAD YOU ARE HERE!

As long as I can remember I was obsessed with makeup not only because of it being colorful but because it is an expression art form. I loved what mascara could do and how a strong lip would make me feel, in today's day and age How often is a bold red lip seen as Too bossy or fierce and bright colors a no-no for chocolaty skin tones. How often have you heard of rules to makeup. Rules to how you can and can not express yourselves , I always was amazed at the icon of a woman Marilyn Monroe was and how she owned all her sexiness and did it all while rocking a solid red lip and liner , in all totality I love makeup and how expressive it can get ,I'm Ayesha Zia a professional and certified makeup artist who hopes to play a small part in changing the conventional beauty standards .
